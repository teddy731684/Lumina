About Us

Crafting Environments
of Timeless Elegance

Since 2014, Lumina Interiors has partnered with discerning clients to create spaces that balance aesthetic ambition with everyday livability. Our multidisciplinary team brings architecture, art, and craft together under one roof — delivering interiors that are as functional as they are beautiful.

Residential Design

Bespoke interiors for homes, apartments, and private estates — spaces that reflect your identity and elevate daily living.

Commercial Interior
